# Tigon Brand Platform

Status: canonical brand direction, approved 2026-07-10.

This document defines the meaning that copy, art direction, section design and motion must support. It does not by itself authorize changes to protected Header/Hero, SEO, URLs, footer/NAP or important links.

## Brand promise

**BYGD FOR Å BLI VALGT.**

This is Tigon's emotional and commercial promise. It is not a phrase to repeat in every section.

## Positioning

Tigon Studio builds high-end websites, apps and digital systems where design, technology and visibility are developed as one whole.

In Norwegian working copy:

> High-end nettsider, apper og digitale systemer hvor design, teknologi og synlighet bygges som én helhet.

The positioning must communicate three things together:

1. **What Tigon builds:** websites, apps and digital systems.
2. **How Tigon builds:** design, technology and visibility as one integrated delivery.
3. **Why it matters:** the result can be found, understood, chosen and measured.

## Result system

**FUNNET. FORSTÅTT. VALGT. MÅLT.**

- **Funnet:** technical SEO, structure and AI-readable content make the solution discoverable.
- **Forstått:** hierarchy, positioning and content make the offer clear.
- **Valgt:** design, trust, flow and conversion make the next step compelling.
- **Målt:** tracking and contact paths show what works and what should improve.

This is the intellectual explanation of the brand promise. It prevents «valgt» from becoming empty advertising copy.

## Visual concept

The brand promise and the visual concept are not the same thing.

- **Brand idea:** `BYGD FOR Å BLI VALGT.`
- **Visual concept:** `SELECTION / FOCUS / DISTINCTION`
- **Motion principle:** elements are prioritised, selected and locked into a precise final state.
- **Editorial journey:** from visibility to choice and measurable effect.

The visual system may express this through contrast, focus, hierarchy, crop, light, scale and precise alignment. It must not become a literal selection-box UI, dashboard, blueprint system or repeated word-highlighting gimmick.

## Non-negotiable distinctions

### «Valgt» is a principle, not decoration

Do not place `VALGT` everywhere. The page must demonstrate why a solution becomes chosen through quality, clarity, visibility and precision.

### «Uklart» is process language, not the brand identity

`Uklart behov`, `Uklart inn. System ut.` and similar wording may remain inside Prosess or supporting conversion copy when it explains how Tigon reduces buyer risk.

`Uklart blir byggbart` must not define the overall brand, visual concept or homepage narrative. The brand is about building digital work that becomes visible, understood, chosen and measurable.

### Arbeid is capability, not portfolio

04 / Arbeid must answer:

> Hva kan Tigon lage for et nytt prosjekt?

It must never answer:

> Hva har Tigon laget før?

Therefore:

- no customer parade, previous-project list or portfolio framing
- no «se hva vi har laget», `Se case` or `Se arkivet` argument inside the section
- no dependence on named previous websites, industries or clients
- no concept surface presented as delivered customer work
- no use of Arbeid as proof that Tigon has built a similar website before
- use capability, demonstration, concept, system, prototype or lab language

The current heading `Dette kan Tigon lage.` is capability framing. It does not replace the global brand promise. Likewise, `BYGD FOR Å BLI VALGT.` may be used as a brand-level moment without turning Arbeid into a past-work showcase.

## Homepage roles

- **Header/Hero:** establish Tigon and explain the concrete offer.
- **Tilnærming:** explain that design, technology and visibility belong together. Existing «uklart» copy is current implementation language, not the future brand premise.
- **Tjenester:** make the deliverables concrete and scannable.
- **Overlevering:** connect launch to continued measurement.
- **Effekt:** own the result system `FUNNET. FORSTÅTT. VALGT. MÅLT.`
- **Arbeid:** show future-facing capabilities and the level Tigon can create.
- **Prosess:** explain how an unclear need becomes a live, measurable system.
- **Manifest:** state the deeper system philosophy without repeating the slogan.
- **Kontakt:** close commercially and calmly; preserved contact/NAP information remains factual.

## Decision test

For every new copy, layout, asset or motion idea, ask:

1. Does this help explain why a Tigon solution becomes found, understood, chosen or measured?
2. Does it show integrated design, technology and visibility?
3. Is it future-facing capability, or has it drifted into past-work portfolio language?
4. Does the visual idea express selection, focus or distinction without becoming a gimmick?
5. Does it still feel premium, editorial, restrained, typographic and near-monochrome?

If the answer is no, the idea does not support the active Tigon brand platform.
