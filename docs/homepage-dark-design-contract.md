# Tigon homepage — current dark baseline

Last reconciled: 2026-07-19.

This file records the currently mounted dark body journey. It is not a visual
preservation contract. Header, Hero, every body chapter and Footer are open to
redesign. `design.md` remains the canonical token authority.

## Principle

The mounted body is a sequence of dark editorial chapters connected by typography,
grid, rhythm, lines and restrained motion. Each root owns a solid semantic
surface. Variation comes from composition, content density and local media,
not a page-wide decorative layer. A redesign may replace this composition and
chapter treatment with a new Tigon clean-room interpretation of Trionn.

## Shared roles

- Display: TGS Perfect for short monumental titles.
- Editorial: JUST Sans for statements, headings and body.
- Meta/actions: Caleb Mono for labels, numbering and controls.
- Surfaces: `surface-deep`, `surface-base`, `surface-raised`, `surface-focus`.
- Text: `text-strong`, `text-medium`, `text-quiet`, `text-faint`.
- Lines: `line-subtle`, `line-default`, `line-strong`.
- Layout: `page-max`, `homepage-gutter`, 12/6 columns and shared rhythm roles.
- Actions: `button-*` roles for filled/outlined controls; the Intro text action
  reuses button typography with a hairline geometry.

No decorative background video, texture, canvas, lighting layer or shader is
part of this contract. Local editorial images remain valid content media.

## Responsive and access contract

- Important text and links are server-rendered.
- Compact layout and motion use matching structural breakpoints.
- Touch, through 768px and reduced motion use native scroll.
- Test 390, 768, 1024 and 1440px; mouse, touch and keyboard; reverse scroll,
  refresh, resize, deep links, reduced motion and no-JS.
- No horizontal overflow, hidden focus or essential text below the token floor.
