# Tigon Work Atmosphere

## Purpose

A silent, seamless background instrument for `04 / Arbeid`. It gives the dark
capability catalogue spatial depth without becoming a separate visual story.

## Concept angle

Selection expressed as slow light moving across a dark material surface: the
frame feels inspected, focused and chosen rather than decorated.

## Visual identity

- Base: `#050706` / `#09100c`
- Cool mineral light: `#8d9d94`
- Warm mauve reflection: `#c8bcc1`
- Deep pine: `#183027`
- No orange, neon, rainbow cycling, text, logos or literal objects
- Large local radial fields instead of a full-screen linear gradient
- Fine grain and restrained vignette bind the layers together

## Motion

- 12 seconds at 30 fps, 1920×1080
- One continuous `slow-breathe / opposing-drift / return` rhythm
- Every animated layer returns to its exact opening state at 12 seconds
- Motion remains broad enough to survive video compression and subtle enough
  to sit behind interactive content

## Website treatment

The loop is not the complete background. The website adds scroll-directed CSS
glows above it so each authored capability pair receives a distinct focus.
Mobile and reduced-motion use a static poster frame.
